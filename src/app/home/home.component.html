About game

A little history

The original game was created in 1970 by Mordecai Meirowitz*. The game was designed for two players. One player became the codemaker, the other one the codebreaker

The codebreaker tried to guess the pattern, in both order and color, within eight to twelve turns.

Break the code

In this game, you will be the codebreaker. You have ten turns to guess the color sequence randomly generated by computer. You have eight colors to choose from and ten turn it figure out the code.

After each turn, you'll be provided with a feedback - based on this, you can adjust your answer.
